Pete Muir

Red Hat, Inc. Edinburgh, Scotland

I lead the [Seam](http://seamframework.org/Seam3), [Weld and CDI TCK](http://seamframework.org/Seam3) projects, am an adviser to the [RichFaces project](http://jboss.org/richfaces), and a founder of the [Arquillian project](http://jboss.org/arquillian). I've worked on a number of specifications including JSF 2.0, AtInject and CDI. I am a regular speaker at JUGs and conferences such as Devoxx (Javapolis), JAX, JavaBlend, JSFDays and JBoss World. I am currently employed by [Red Hat Inc.](http://redhat.com) working on [JBoss](http://jboss.org) open source projects. Before working for Red Hat, I used and contributed to Seam whilst working at a UK based staffing agency as IT Development Manager.
